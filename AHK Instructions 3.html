AUTOHOTKEY

Scripts to make your life better.

AutoHotkey is a very powerful scripting language that allows you to control your computer and automate redundant tasks.

I've created many different scripts that have specific uses, so you may be overwhelmed at first.
You should focus on just a couple until they become second nature, at which point you can try expanding to the others.

Don't be afraid of the scripts! They are here to help you!

You are likely pretty set in your ways, but try to be flexible and adapt the scripts to your workflow.

You can always come back to this webpage for a quick explanation of each script.

There is also an interactive tutorial available which I'll link later.

QUICK DATE AND INITIALS

You can save 2,000 keystrokes a day with this!

Default Keybinding (Shift + CapsLock)

Quick Date and Initials will quickly add your current Remarks Date and Remarks Username into docketing.

There are three ways to use this function:

1) When used on a blank line, the date and your username will be typed, and the cursor will jump to the center for quick typing.
2) When placed at the beginning of a line of remarks, the text will be encapsulated by the date and your username.
3) Remarks longer than one line in length can be completely selected. When the shortcut is used, the remarks will be encapsulated between the date and your username. This will also automatically remove the * - that occasionally generates automatically on actions.
***Be sure to include punctuation to customize the appearance of your remarks! (e.g. "12/14/16 -" and "-FLASTNAME")

SYSTEM SWITCH

Quickly jump between Live and Audit Trail systems!

Default Keybinding (Control + ~ -OR- Shortcuts Bar)

System Switch allows you to quickly switch between the Live and Audit Trail systems with the press of a button.

This can take you from a Live Trademark or Patent main screen to an AT Trademark or AT Patent main screen and vice versa.
It can also take you from a Live Trademark or Patent Action to an AT Trademark or AT Patent main screen and vice versa.

Unfortunately, switching from a Live Action to an AT Action or vice versa isn't possible.
This also doesn't work from the Invention screen and would be difficult to implement.

Consider using the Record Opener to jump to directly to a main screen.

The Settings/Shortcuts Bar is a quick way to return to the Settings Menu, and is useful as an alternate way to use the other available functions.

STORE/PASTE CLIPBOARDS

Need to repeatedly paste separate things? There's a script for that!

Default Keybinding (Copy: Ctrl + 5, Paste: Ctrl + 6 -OR- Shortcuts Bar)

This option allows you store additional clipboards (i.e. Ctrl + C) for pasting at a later time while still utilizing the default Ctrl + C shortcut.
This works best with shorter, simple text without images or hyperlinks.

How to Copy:
1) Select the desired text you would like to store.
2) Press Ctrl + 5 (or use the Shortcuts Bar). A menu you will appear with several available slots.
3) Choose a slot to store the selected text. The menu will disappear and your text will be stored for later use.

How to Paste:
1) When you're ready to paste stored text, press Ctrl + 6 (or use the Shortcuts Bar). A menu will pop-up showing you your stored text.
2) Click where you'd like to paste the text.
3) Click on the slot with your desired text, and it will get typed as if you pasted it with Ctrl + V.

The Copy/Paste windows are movable and will stay where placed. You can also choose a custom color for your windows on the Settings Menu.

HYPERLINK FROM EXCEL

Open hyperlinks to docketing directly from Excel!

Default Keybinding (Control + 1 -OR- Shortcuts Bar)

This script allows you to open hyperlinks from Excel directly into docketing. This typically isn't possible due to limitations of Excel.

This function is mostly for Master Docket, *IntFees, and *Abandonment for running Facing and Past Due reports.

Most users won't need to use this option, but it is available just in case.

You will most likely leave this option disabled unless you have a reason to enable it.

Each will be described in its own section below.

MULTIUSE - CALENDAR EXTENDER

Quickly extend a date by certain durations of time!

Default Keybinding (Ctrl + Right Click)

Calendar Extender allows you to quickly extend a date in docketing by certain intervals of time (1W, 2W, 1M, 2M, 3M, 6M, 1Y).

If auto-remarks are turned on, remarks will be automatically added, noting the date, the duration of the extension, and your username.
Only the Responsible person's initials need to be added.

You can also use the calendar to choose a specific date if you don't feel like typing.

MULTIUSE - REMARKS SUGGESTER

Quit typing the same remarks over and over and over and over again!

Default Keybinding (Ctrl + Right Click)

Remarks Suggester is functional but barebones.Selecting an action will provide typical remarks to paste.

***Currently only works on Status Checks and IN Corresponding Applications Actions.***

If you have suggestions or requests, let me know! It literally takes five seconds to add new ones.

You won't even need to restart the script!

MULTIUSE - ATTORNEY FINDER

Need to email the entire prosecution team? Check this out!
No more 2JCS/2JSC mistypes or Ethical Wall issues!

Default Keybinding (Ctrl + Right Click)

Attorney Finder allows you to select a block of attorneys in docketing.

It will then remove the duplicates, substitute irregular names, and add your AT partner to your Clipboard.

You can then paste into the CC field and move the responsible person to the To field.

This is really great for when your AT partner changes. It will prevent you from emailing the wrong person on accident.

You'll still need to include the usual suspects like *IntFees, *IntExams, *Invoices, *Docketing LC, *IntlClerksDocketing, etc.

MULTIUSE - RECORD OPENER

Quickly open Client Codes from anywhere you can select them!

Default Keybinding (Ctrl + Right Click)
Quick Record Opener (Alt + Right Click/Left Click)

-Record Opener will open a Client Code directly into docketing.
You can customize which menu options appear on the pop-up, or force Patent/TM only.
This works anywhere you can select text (Outlook, Excel, an email, etc.) and is VERY quick.

-Quick Record Opener ignores your other settings and is always functional.
You can customize which button opens Patent and which opens Trademark.

EFS TOOLS

Multiple tools for all your EFS needs!

EFS CERTIFICATE AND PASSWORD FINDER

(Default Keybindings: !ATY)

This is used when signing into EFS for processing an assigned QC.
Typing !ATY will auto-fill the certificate's location, and then find and enter the password into EFS.
This is especially useful when the information isn't specifically provided when the QC is assigned.
***This can occasionally fail. Simply try again. If you encounter a missing 2ATY team or incorrect password, let Tyler know.

EFS INFORMATION FILLER

(Default Keybindings: Windows + E -OR- Quick Settings Menu)
This will auto-fill the information to access the assigned EFS ID.
When on the EFS information screen, the screen will jump a bit, and it should fill in the fields.
***This may fail the first time. Access the URL in IE and delete everything from the ? over.
Press Enter to reload the page, then try running it again.

EFS FEES LOGIN


(Default Keybindings: Windows + F -OR- Quick Settings Menu)
This allows you to premptively sign into the EFS Fees system so it won't log you out every fifteen minutes.
***I suggest running this two minutes prior to completing a QC, maybe while you save your documents.
You should be logged by the time you submit, and you will avoid the log-in screen.

I'd show you a picture, but it isn't easy to really represent how it works. Just try it out. If it doesn't work, you just have to do it the old-fashioned way.
